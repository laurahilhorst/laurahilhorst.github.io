About me

My name -as you may have guessed- is Laura Hilhorst. I'm an Amsterdam based Information Studies student. These past 8 weeks I've taken a course called Multimedia and Interaction design, during which I have gained some designing skills.This project has sparked my interests in both design and how humans behave online. In the future, I hope to be able to extend my knowledge and skill in this field, a journey of which this website will be my outlet.

About this website

This website is the final project of my multimedia and interaction design course. On it, you'll find all of the work I've produced in the past eight weeks. The Deliverables-page holds my finished and graded works. The Dump-section holds all of my preparative work, trains of thoughts and doodles to illustrate my creative processes.
